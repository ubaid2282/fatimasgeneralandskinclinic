Hair Transplant

Hair transplant is a minimally invasive surgical technique where hair follicles are extracted from a dense hair area (usually the back of the scalp) and transplanted into bald or thinning areas.The procedure is done under local anesthesia, and patients can usually go home the same day.

FUE Hair Transplant

Currently the most popular technique. • Individual hair follicles are extracted directly using a micro punch tool. • No strip removal. • No linear scar (tiny dot scars only). • Faster healing and less discomfort. ✅ Advantage: Minimal scarring, faster recovery
